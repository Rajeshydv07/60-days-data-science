Customer Retention Strategy

From Predictive Analytics to Actionable Insights

Prepared by: Data Science & Analytics Team | July 2026

The Business Challenge

We are losing customers at an unsustainable rate, primarily driven by factors within our control.

26% Avg. Churn Rate
$12M Annual Revenue at Risk
88% Churn from M2M Contracts

Key Drivers of Customer Churn

  • The Commitment Gap: Month-to-Month contracts represent our biggest vulnerability. Customers treat the service as a utility.
  • The Onboarding Danger Zone: The highest risk of cancellation occurs in the first 6 months (Tenure).
  • The Missing "Sticky" Services: Customers without Tech Support or Online Security are significantly more likely to leave.

Strategic Action Plan

1. Launch "Commit & Save" Initiative

Target the top 20% high-risk Month-to-Month users with a 15% discount to upgrade to an annual contract.

2. The "First 90 Days" Program

Implement proactive, white-glove onboarding for new acquisitions to navigate the high-risk early tenure period.

3. Value-Add Service Bundling

Redesign our pricing architecture to embed Tech Support and Security as standard, non-optional features.

Immediate Next Steps

We are requesting approval to launch a pilot of the "Commit & Save" campaign to a 5% holdout group next week.

This will allow us to measure conversion rates and validate the financial impact model before a full-scale Q4 rollout.
