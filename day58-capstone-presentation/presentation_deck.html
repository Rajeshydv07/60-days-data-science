Customer Intelligence Platform (CIP)

Predictive Analytics for Customer Retention

Presented by: Data Science & Analytics Team

Day 58: Capstone Demo & Presentation

The Business Problem

Customer acquisition costs (CAC) are rising, yet customer churn remains unchecked.

  • We are losing valuable customers without warning.
  • Customer Success teams are acting reactively, not proactively.
  • No unified view of Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) and Churn Risk.

The Solution: CIP

An end-to-end Machine Learning platform that predicts churn before it happens and provides actionable retention strategies in real-time.

Analytics & Engineering Workflow

From Raw Data to Real-Time Insights.

The Pipeline

  • Data Engineering: Scikit-Learn pipelines handling imputation, scaling, and one-hot encoding without data leakage.
  • Model Training: Optimized Random Forest and Logistic Regression models focusing on PR-AUC.
  • Service Layer: Asynchronous REST API powered by FastAPI.
  • Presentation: Interactive Streamlit Dashboard for stakeholders.

Forecasting & Segmentation

  • Survival Analysis: Identifying the "First 90 Days Vulnerability".
  • Value Segmentation: Grouping customers into High, Medium, and Low MRR tiers.
  • Contract Risk: Pinpointing the high volatility in Month-to-Month contracts.

Real-Time Prediction Module

Empowering Customer Success Agents.

How it Works

  1. Agent inputs customer details into the Streamlit UI.
  2. FastAPI processes the data through the pickled ML pipeline.
  3. Model returns a Churn Probability Score and Risk Tier.
  4. Explainable AI (SHAP): Shows exactly why the model made its prediction (e.g., "Lack of Tech Support").

Business Insights & Outcomes

Key Insights

  • Month-to-month contracts are the #1 driver of churn.
  • Customers using bundled "sticky" services (Tech Support, Online Security) churn 40% less.
  • Early intervention (Days 0-90) is critical for long-term retention.
